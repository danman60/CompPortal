Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register dancers for a competition?

Go to the Dancers section, click "Add New Dancer" to register your dancers, then create a reservation for your chosen competition and add entries for each routine.

What information do I need for competition registration?

You'll need dancer details (name, date of birth, dance style, experience level), agent information, and signed waivers for liability and age consent.

How do I export schedules and reports?

Visit the Reports section where you can export competition schedules, dancer lists, and other reports in CSV or PDF format.

Can I edit dancer information after registration?

Yes, you can edit dancer profiles anytime from the Dancers section. Use the edit button on any dancer card to update their information.

What are the competition fees?

Competition fees vary by location: Toronto Regional ($85/entry), Hamilton Championship ($95/entry), Mississauga Spring Classic ($80/entry). Fees are calculated automatically when you create a reservation.

User Guides

Adding Dancers

  1. 1. Navigate to the Dancers page
  2. 2. Click "Add New Dancer" button
  3. 3. Fill out dancer information form
  4. 4. Submit to save to your studio roster

Creating Reservations

  1. 1. Go to Reservations → New Reservation
  2. 2. Select competition location
  3. 3. Enter number of entry spaces needed
  4. 4. Fill agent details and sign waivers

Managing Entries

  1. 1. From Reservations page, click "Manage Entries"
  2. 2. Select dancer and routine details
  3. 3. Choose category, style, and level
  4. 4. Add music duration and special notes

Exporting Reports

  1. 1. Navigate to the Reports section
  2. 2. Choose export format (CSV or PDF)
  3. 3. Select report type (Schedule, Dancers, etc.)
  4. 4. Download file to your device
