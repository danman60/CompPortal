COMPUTITION - A Quick Notes Guide

Creating Your Account

  • Click on “Register” at the top right of the screen
  • Fill out all of the fields, check the box to indicate that you have accepted the terms and conditions, and click “Register”.

Note: Once you have created an account, simply click the login link in the top right hand side of the screen and enter your user name and password.

Editing Your Profile

You may change your user name, email address or password by clicking “Edit Profile” at the top left hand side of the screen when logged on.

My Dashboard

Once you have logged in, you will be taken to the "My Dashboard" page. You can return to this page at any time by clicking the link located at the top right hand side of the screen. The first step you need to take is to enter your Studio and Dancer information.

Studio Information

  • Click on “New Studio”
  • Enter all required fields and click on “Create Studio”
  • If all of the information you entered is correct, click on Dancers at the bottom of the screen. You may also Edit your studio information or return to the studio listing if you wish

Dancers

  • Once you have clicked on “Dancers”, you need to enter your first dancer’s first name, last name and birthdate. It does not matter which order you enter your dancers in. Simply enter all dancers who will perform at this competition
  • Keep creating new dancers using the link at the bottom of the screen until all dancers have been entered
  • Once all dancers have been entered, click on “My Dashboard” at the top of the screen

Editing Studio and Dancer Information

  • From “My Dashboard”, click on “Studio & Dancer Information”
  • All of your studios will be listed on this screen. If you click on any of the links under “Actions” on the far right of the screen, you will be able to view, edit and delete all information listed in your account

Creating Entries

  • From "My Dashboard", click on “Entry Information”
  • Click “New Entry” at the bottom left of the screen
  • Fill out each field with the appropriate information for one of your entries. When you select the dancers at the bottom of the screen, only select the dancers associated with that specific performance
  • Click on “Create Entry”
  • Repeat this process until all entries have been submitted

Note: the entry fee and average age for the entry will be calculated and updated automatically each time you save the entry. This information is shown on the entry listing and entry details page.

Editing Entries

  • From “My Dashboard”, click on “Entry Information”
  • All of your entries that have been submitted are listed on this screen. You may choose to view, edit or delete any entry using the links under the “Actions” column on the far right of the screen
